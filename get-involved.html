Get Involved

If you want to get more involved contact Jeanne Young. I'm sure she could use the help! Here are some ideas on how to help.

Volunteer for the Gala

The Gala needs people to set-up, check guests in, take people's money for silent auction items (sorry, no keeping the money but it will go to fund vital research intiatives so that should make you happy), etc.

Silent Auction

Speaking of the silent auction, it needs items to be auctioned. Any ideas? Connections? Know someone who owns a Bed and Breakfast or lives next to a sports star? And of course you can always help by bidding on items!

Volunteer for Future Events

The Childhood Brain Tumor Foundation has more events to help with or participate in. Get more information about events and fundraisers such as our Superheroes 5k by visiting the CBTF website.

Donate

We always appriciate your donations. Donations are completely tax-deductible and help fund reaserach, increase public awareness and improve quality of life for those affected. To donate visit childhoodbraintumor.org.
