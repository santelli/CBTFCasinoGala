Event Details

You are cordially invited to the Childhood Brain Tumor Foundation's 2014 Casino Gala! The Gala will take place on Saturday, November 22nd.

Location

The Manor Country Club is located at 14901 Carlton Road, Rockville, MD 20853.

There will be an open bar with beer and wine, hours d'vours, and a buffet dinner.

Live music, dancing, silent auction, raffel, and, of course, casino games. This year's band will be The Giant Flying Turtles!

Attire

The attire is upscale casual to cocktail to formal.
