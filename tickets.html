Tickets

RSVP by November 13th. Regular tickets are $100, Patron tickets are $140, and Sponsor tickets are $250. Children get a special price, email cbtrf@aol.com for more information. Your tickets will be waiting at the door if you don't recieve them ahead of time.

Online

You can pay by credit card (MasterCard, VISA, or American Express) by going to the support page on the CBTF website and using our GIVE online button. Put in the notes that you would like tickets for the Casino Gala, how many tickets, and each person's name.

By Mail

You can send payment to the Childhood Brain Tumor Foundation at 20312 Watkins Meadow Drive, Germantown, Maryland 20876.
